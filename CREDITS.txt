# Credits and Attributions

The Insane Tweaks SRPWIZ project wouldn't be possible without the foundational work, APIs, and inspiration from the following mods and their creators:

## Core Frameworks and Integration
- **Electroblob's Wizardry** by Electroblob: The central wizardry and mana mechanics.
- **Ancient Spellcraft** by Windanesz: The Battlemage equipment base, Runic shields, and Arcane Workbench logic.
- **Spartan Weaponry** by oblivioussp: The comprehensive weapon property and combat API.

## Content and Inspiration
- **Scape and Run: Parasites** by Dhanantry and the SRP Team: The parasitic evolution and world-building concepts.
- **Scape and Spartan: Parasites** by Raptor and the SWParasites Team: Parasitic material integration.
- **Enigmatic Legacy** by Aizistra and KELETU: Inspiration for Aegis shield mechanics and synergy systems.
- **Bountiful Baubles** by CursedFlames: Inspiration for specific item mechanics.
- **PotionCore** by Tmtravlr: The Cure and status cleansing mechanics.

## Assets and Tools
- **Electroblob's Wizardry Team**: Battlemage set texture bases.
- **CleanroomMC & GroovyScript Teams**: Tools for performance optimization and initial prototyping.
- **Faithful Team**: "Core" items textures were created using modified assets originally from the Faithful 32x Resource Pack. Distributed in accordance with their community license.
